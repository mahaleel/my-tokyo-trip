Narita Arrival → Shinjuku

1
After customs, follow JR Line / Narita Express signs
2
Go down escalator/elevator to train station level
3
Find JR EAST counter or green ticket machine
4
Buy Narita Express ticket to Shinjuku Station
5
Choose ordinary reserved seat — check platform/car/seat number carefully
6
Board Narita Express to Shinjuku (~1hr 20min)
7
Arrive at Shinjuku Station → JR East Exit
8
Use GO App or Uber taxi to APA Hotel — recommended with luggage on first night

First Tokyo night + luggage + Shinjuku can feel overwhelming — taxi is the right call. Backup stations: Seibu Shinjuku Station or Higashi-Shinjuku Station.

How to Find the JCB Lounge at Narita T2

1
Enter NAIA Terminal 1 → check in baggage → pass immigration → pass final security
2
Walk toward the boarding gates — go to International Departure Area (Airside)
3
Head to the East Concourse
4
Located next to Gate 2 / across the Gate 2 area
5
Present: Metrobank Travel Visa Signature or eligible JCB card + boarding pass + passport

JCB Lounge Tips

For a solo traveler arriving at Narita around 11:00 AM for a 2:25 PM flight — very comfortable timing, not rushed but not excessively early.

Use the lounge mainly to:

1
Have brunch / lunch
2
Charge everything to 100%
3
Use the restroom before security
4
Organize receipts and tax-free purchases

This avoids buying expensive airport food afterward. Cards accepted: Metrobank Travel Visa Signature or eligible JCB card.
